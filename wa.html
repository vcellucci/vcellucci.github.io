Wizard's Apprentice

Introducing Wizard’s Apprentice—the ultimate digital companion for your next round of Wizard! Leave the paper score sheets behind and step into a realm of convenience. With Wizard’s Apprentice, you can effortlessly track scores, bids, and rounds all in one intuitive interface. Whether you’re a seasoned sorcerer or a first-time Wizard player, let this handy app do the heavy lifting so you can focus on mastering every trick and triumph. Grab your deck, gather your friends, and let Wizard’s Apprentice make your game night truly magical!
